A Doctor Who's Been There

He Climbs Mountains on the Weekend. He'll Help You Climb Back From Pain.

Dr. Spencer Hiebert grew up in Aledo, Texas with a deep love for the outdoors. Hiking, skiing, climbing, paddleboarding — staying active isn't just a hobby for him. It's a way of life. And it's what drives his passion for helping patients get back to the activities that matter most to them.

After earning his Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology from Texas A&M University, Dr. Hiebert went on to complete his Doctor of Chiropractic degree at Parker University in Dallas. Over the past five and a half years, he's treated patients across the Dallas-Fort Worth area and Colorado — athletes, working parents, seniors, desk workers, and everyone in between.

His approach is evidence-based, thorough, and deeply personal. He believes that healthcare should be welcoming and accessible to everyone, and that every patient deserves to understand their body, their condition, and their path forward.

"At Summit, my mission is simple: help you move better, feel better, and get back to living life at full elevation."

Our Story

Move better. Feel better. Live without limits.

Dr. Spencer Hiebert grew up in Aledo, Texas with a deep love for the outdoors. Hiking, skiing, climbing, paddleboarding — staying active isn't just a hobby for him. It's a way of life. And it's what drives his passion for helping patients get back to the activities that matter most to them.

After earning his Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology from Texas A&M University, Dr. Hiebert completed his Doctor of Chiropractic degree at Parker University in Dallas. Over the past five and a half years, he has treated patients across the Dallas–Fort Worth area and Colorado — athletes, working parents, seniors, desk workers, and everyone in between.

His approach is evidence-based, thorough, and deeply personal. He believes healthcare should be welcoming and accessible, and that every patient deserves to understand their body, their condition, and their path forward.

"At Summit, my mission is simple: help you move better, feel better, and get back to living life at full elevation."

The Summit Philosophy

Most chiropractic offices get you in, get you adjusted, and get you out. Summit was built differently. Every relationship starts with a real conversation about your history, lifestyle, goals, and what has not worked before.

From there, Dr. Hiebert builds a treatment plan specific to you — combining hands-on chiropractic with advanced modalities like spinal decompression, rehabilitative exercise, dry needling, red light therapy, and infrared sauna recovery — all in one modern Colleyville clinic.

Whether your goal is recovering from injury, improving performance, managing chronic pain, or staying active as you age — we are here for the long game.

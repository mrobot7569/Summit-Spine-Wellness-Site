Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Summit Spine & Wellness (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

Information we collect

When you submit an appointment or contact form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, preferred service, preferred date/time, and any message you provide. Server logs and analytics may collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

How we use information

  • To respond to appointment and inquiry requests
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website
  • To comply with legal obligations

Health information

This website is not a portal for protected health information (PHI). Please do not submit sensitive medical records through public forms. Clinical documentation is handled through appropriate clinic systems once you become a patient.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the site or process forms, or when required by law.

Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site function and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your choices

To update or delete inquiry data, or ask questions about this policy, contact us using the details on our Contact page.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when revisions are posted.
